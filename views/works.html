Professional Career

After being the first overall pick of 2003 NBA Draft, James became the third player in league history to average at least 20 points, 5 rebounds, and 5 assists per game as a rookie. In the first two seasons Lebron and his team failed to make playoffs but after that Lebron was in every playoff for the next 12 years.
